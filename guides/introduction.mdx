**Equisettle is a modern B2B Accounts Receivable (A/R) platform that helps businesses manage invoices, payments, and customer balances effortlessly.**  
It simplifies how companies track receivables, reconcile payments, and stay on top of their cash flow, all in one place.

## Who is it for?

- **Finance teams** looking to simplify collections and payment tracking.
- **Developers** who want to integrate A/R data into internal dashboards or ERP tools.
- **B2B companies** that need a reliable way to manage invoices and customer accounts at scale.
